Help - Productivity App

How to Set a Timer

Select the desired time from the dropdown menus. Once you've selected the time, click the "Set Timer" button. When you're ready to start the timer, click the green "Start" button.

How to Add a Task

Enter the task details in the input field below the "Task List" header. You can also choose a date for the task. Once you've entered the details, click the "Add" button, or press the "Enter" key, to add the task to the list.

How to View the Task Calendar

You can view your tasks in a calendar format by clicking the "View Task Calendar" link at the bottom of the main page. This will show you all your tasks organized by their assigned dates.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I delete a task?
A: To delete a task, click the "X" button next to the task.

Q: Can I edit a task?
A: Currently, tasks cannot be edited once added. You can delete the task and create a new one with the updated details.
